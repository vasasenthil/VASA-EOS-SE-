`module.yaml` (schema below) and its implementation. This catalogue is the canonical partition; a build-time
linter generates the full list from `modules/*/module.yaml` and verifies every module is declared, owned, and
compliance-tagged (§3, §12). Status vocabulary: `spec-only` → `scaffold` → `implemented` (Definition of Done §25).
